# Apple Financial Analysis & 3-Year Forecast: Executive Summary

## Project Objective
This project evaluates Apple Inc.'s historical financial performance from FY2021 through FY2025 and builds a 3-year scenario forecast for FY2026 through FY2028. The analysis focuses on revenue growth, profitability, liquidity, free cash flow generation, balance sheet strength, and future financial outlook.

## Data Sources
The analysis uses public Apple financial statement data from SEC filings and Apple investor materials. All financial values are presented in millions of U.S. dollars unless otherwise stated.

## Historical Performance Summary
Apple's revenue increased from $365.8B in FY2021 to $416.2B in FY2025. Revenue declined in FY2023 but recovered in FY2024 and reached a new high in FY2025.

Gross margin improved from 41.8% in FY2021 to 46.9% in FY2025, indicating stronger profitability, improved business mix, and pricing resilience. Operating income increased from $108.9B in FY2021 to $133.1B in FY2025, showing that Apple maintained strong operating leverage despite revenue fluctuations.

Net income increased from $94.7B in FY2021 to $112.0B in FY2025. Although net income declined in FY2024, it rebounded strongly in FY2025.

## Cash Flow Summary
Apple continued to generate strong free cash flow throughout the analysis period. Free cash flow was $98.8B in FY2025, supporting continued reinvestment, shareholder returns, and financial flexibility.

## Balance Sheet Summary
Apple maintained a large asset base and significant liquidity. However, liabilities remained high relative to shareholders' equity, making leverage and liquidity metrics important areas to monitor.

## Forecast Methodology
The forecast model uses FY2025 revenue as the baseline and applies scenario-based assumptions for revenue growth, gross margin, operating margin, net margin, capital expenditures as a percentage of revenue, and free cash flow margin.

Three scenarios were created:

- **Base Case:** Moderate growth with stable margin performance.
- **Upside Case:** Stronger revenue growth, margin expansion, and higher free cash flow conversion.
- **Downside Case:** Slower revenue growth, margin pressure, and lower free cash flow conversion.

## Key Forecast Takeaway
Under the base case, Apple is projected to continue growing revenue and maintaining strong profitability through FY2028. The upside case shows stronger revenue and free cash flow potential if growth accelerates and margins expand. The downside case highlights the risk of slower revenue growth and margin compression.

## Business Recommendation
Apple appears financially strong based on historical profitability, cash flow generation, and scale. Future monitoring should focus on revenue growth drivers, product and services mix, margin sustainability, capital allocation, leverage, and macroeconomic risks affecting consumer demand.
