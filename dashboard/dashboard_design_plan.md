# Dashboard Design Plan: Apple Financial Analysis & Forecast

## Dashboard Objective
The objective of this dashboard is to provide an executive-level view of Apple Inc.'s historical financial performance, profitability trends, cash flow strength, balance sheet position, and 3-year forecast outlook.

## Dashboard Tool
Power BI or Tableau

## Data Sources
- `data/processed/apple_financials_with_ratios.csv`
- `data/processed/apple_forecast_output.csv`
- `data/processed/apple_forecast_assumptions.csv`

## Page 1: Executive Summary

### KPI Cards
- Revenue
- Net Income
- Operating Income
- Free Cash Flow
- Gross Margin %
- Operating Margin %
- Net Margin %

### Visuals
- Revenue trend by fiscal year
- Net income trend by fiscal year
- Free cash flow trend by fiscal year
- Profitability margin trend

### Business Purpose
This page gives executives a quick view of Apple’s financial health and performance direction.

## Page 2: Profitability Analysis

### KPI Cards
- Gross Margin %
- Operating Margin %
- Net Margin %
- Return on Assets %
- Return on Equity %

### Visuals
- Gross margin trend
- Operating margin trend
- Net margin trend
- Revenue vs operating income

### Business Purpose
This page evaluates Apple’s ability to convert revenue into profit.

## Page 3: Balance Sheet & Liquidity

### KPI Cards
- Total Assets
- Total Liabilities
- Shareholders' Equity
- Current Ratio
- Debt-to-Equity Ratio

### Visuals
- Assets vs liabilities trend
- Shareholders' equity trend
- Current ratio trend
- Debt-to-equity trend

### Business Purpose
This page evaluates Apple’s financial position, liquidity, and leverage.

## Page 4: Cash Flow Analysis

### KPI Cards
- Operating Cash Flow
- Capital Expenditures
- Free Cash Flow
- Free Cash Flow Margin %

### Visuals
- Operating cash flow trend
- Free cash flow trend
- CapEx as % of revenue
- Free cash flow margin trend

### Business Purpose
This page evaluates Apple’s cash generation and capital efficiency.

## Page 5: Forecast Scenario Analysis

### KPI Cards
- Forecast Revenue
- Forecast Net Income
- Forecast Free Cash Flow
- Forecast FCF Margin %

### Visuals
- Revenue forecast by scenario
- Net income forecast by scenario
- Free cash flow forecast by scenario
- Base vs upside vs downside comparison

### Business Purpose
This page helps evaluate Apple’s future financial outlook under different business assumptions.

## Recommended Dashboard Filters
- Fiscal Year
- Scenario
- Metric Category

## Final Dashboard Story
Apple demonstrates strong profitability, consistent cash flow generation, and resilient financial performance. The scenario forecast provides a forward-looking view of revenue, earnings, and free cash flow under base, upside, and downside assumptions.
