# Data Source Notes

## Company
Apple Inc. (AAPL)

## Dataset
This project uses Apple's annual financial statement data from fiscal years 2021 through 2025.

## Unit
All financial values are shown in millions of U.S. dollars, except diluted EPS.

## Main Metrics Collected
- Revenue
- Gross profit
- Operating income
- Net income
- Total assets
- Total liabilities
- Shareholders' equity
- Current assets
- Current liabilities
- Operating cash flow
- Capital expenditures
- Free cash flow
- Diluted EPS

## Free Cash Flow Formula
Free Cash Flow = Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures

## Sources
- Apple Inc. Form 10-K, Fiscal Year 2025
- Apple Inc. Form 10-K, Fiscal Year 2023
- Apple FY2021 Q4 Consolidated Financial Statements

## Notes
Capital expenditures are shown as positive cash outflows in the cleaned dataset.
